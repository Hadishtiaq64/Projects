About Us

We are a student-run organization focused on aerospace engineering, payload research, propulsion, avionics, and flight operations. Our mission is to provide hands-on experience through the full lifecycle of high-power rocketry projects and competitions.

What We Do

  • Design and build composite airframes
  • Develop avionics and flight software
  • Integrate scientific payloads
  • Test propulsion and recovery systems

Who Can Join

All disciplines are welcome. Whether you love structures, electronics, software, science, or outreach—there is a role for you.
